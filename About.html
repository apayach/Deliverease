ABOUT US

DeliverEase aims to improve student campus life by providing on-demand meal delivery service around campus. Students no longer have to go through the hassle of getting healthy meal conveniently. DeliverEase will help build a strong student-to-student community as well as increasing the university experience and safety. By focusing the delivery service on the parameters of campus, DeliverEase would help solve the issue of opportunity cost, as well as improving student’s health, convenience, and safety.
